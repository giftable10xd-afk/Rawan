Commission-Based Partnership

A performance-based model where we sell your products at the best market price and share results transparently.

  • 1We sign a formal contract with the exporter
  • 2Exporter ships products to Kuwait
  • 3We display in showrooms & distribute to supermarkets
  • 4Products sold at best possible market price
  • 5Total sales calculated after full container is sold
